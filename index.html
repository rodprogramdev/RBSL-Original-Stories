ABOUT ME

I am Rodrigo Bonzerr S. Lopez a web designer, musician and I like writing original stories.

Writing helps me exercise my innovative skills and taps my innate desire to touch lives using the stories that I write. I also love writing original music and I am also a very enthusiastic person who loves learning.

I have fifteen years in the field of teaching with experience in creating modules and manuals for trainings in topics such as safety, computer literacy, emergency, and disaster preparedness. I also managed both personal and client Wordpress websites.

I am an author of the book "Practical Self-defense 2009", and was a co-author of the book of Grandmaster Frank Soberino, "WEDO Traditional Arnis Secrets". I also have written a free e-book titled "Introduction to Git and Github."
